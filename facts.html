Did You Know?

  • Fact 1: Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.
  • Fact 2: A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus. It takes Venus longer to rotate on its axis than to complete one orbit of the Sun.
  • Fact 3: Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.
  • Fact 4: The Eiffel Tower can be 15 cm taller during the summer due to the expansion of the metal in the heat.
  • Fact 5: Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not. Botanically, a berry is a fruit produced from the ovary of a single flower with seeds embedded in the flesh.
  • Fact 6: The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar on August 27, 1896. Zanzibar surrendered after 38 minutes.
  • Fact 7: There are more possible iterations of a game of chess than there are atoms in the known universe.
  • Fact 8: The largest living organism is a fungus in Oregon that covers 2,385 acres and is estimated to be over 2,400 years old.
