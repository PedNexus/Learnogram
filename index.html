About Us

PedNexus is an innovative platform designed to enhance the educational experience through digital technologies. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and effective learning environment that supports students and educators in their pursuit of knowledge.

Features

Our platform offers a range of features to support modern education:

  • Recording (video, audio)
  • Uploading (partial)
  • Scanning camera
  • Specific Lighting
  • Integration with smart board
  • Accessing (partial)
  • Doubt clearing method
  • VR
  • Transcripts - materials
  • Autosuggestions
  • Adding Practical clips
  • Personal Learning AI
  • Smart suggestion
  • Q&A
  • Adaptive assessment and progress tracking
  • Collaborative learning space
  • Smart notification and reminder
  • Talent showcasing platform
